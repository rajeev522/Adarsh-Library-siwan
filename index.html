The Story Behind Adarsh Library

Adarsh Library was founded in 2025, inspired by the vision of Mr. Kailash Gupta. He believed in creating dedicated spaces that support learning and personal growth.

Our Mission

At Adarsh Library, our mission is to provide a peaceful, well-equipped, and inspiring environment. We want to help people focus on their studies. We are committed to maintaining a clean, organized, and distraction-free space. This is where students and professionals can achieve their academic and career goals.

Our Vision

We provide quality facilities, a supportive atmosphere, and a dedication to excellence. We aim to be the premier study destination in Siwan. Adarsh Library is dedicated to fostering a productive learning environment for everyone. We want to provide a space that supports your academic and professional aspirations.
